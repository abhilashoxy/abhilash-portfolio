Who I Am

I’ve spent the last 9+ years working in the IT industry as a full-stack developer, mainly with the Microsoft stack: .NET / C#, Angular, SQL Server. I enjoy taking a problem that looks messy and designing a clean, maintainable solution around it.

Most of my experience is around internal tools, dashboards, line-of-business apps, and backend APIs that support real users and real workflows. I care about clarity, reliability and long-term maintainability more than just “making it work”.

Alongside my full-time roles, I’m now focusing on freelance work and long-term collaborations where I can own the technical side of a project end-to-end for startups and businesses.
